Oblivion Review

HALO 3 ODST THOUGHTS/DISSCUSION

The music is depressing with its slow saxaphones and pianos but when there is action there is electronic guitar songs that I'm not a huge fan of, Bungie focused more of thier efforts on depressing and lonley atmosphere and less on
action orchestrol like music, and that's why I love it. The game dosent put you in the shoes of a dude who's obviously going to win at the end of the day,
there's uncertianty to the game through story and atmosphere with dark lit areas, well writen characters, and amazing music..
